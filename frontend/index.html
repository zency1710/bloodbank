About BloodBank

Dedicated to connecting blood donors with those in need, creating a community where every donation saves lives.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients by providing a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly platform that ensures blood is available when and where it's needed most.

We envision a world where no one dies due to blood shortage, where every community has access to safe blood, and where the act of donation becomes a celebrated part of our shared humanity.

Donor Registration

Donation Requirements

  • Age between 18-65 years
  • Weight minimum 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Good general health
  • No recent illness or medication

Benefits of Donating

  • Save up to 3 lives with one donation
  • Free health checkup and screening
  • Boost your cardiovascular health
  • Experience the joy of giving

Did You Know?

Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood. Your donation can help accident victims, cancer patients, surgical patients, and those with blood disorders. Be a hero in someone's story.
